Homework Problem

One of the surprising symptoms in many COVID-19 patients is the unusual increase in the number of blood clots, which can result in potentially fatal events such as pumonary emboli. This data file contains the differential expression analysis of the platelet cells from a small cohort of COVID-19 patients vs. normal, non-infected volunteers. Use Cytoscape to explore this data and provide at least two images that might suggest why there is an increase in clots in COVID-19 patients. The answer isn't definitive, even in the analysis provided by the original paper, so don't expect a "smoking gun" here. For simplicity only consider fold changes > 2 and < -2. You may also want to separately analyze up-regulated and down-regulated proteins, although keep in mind that sometimes the combination can be important.
