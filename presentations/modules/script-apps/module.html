Regular apps for Cytoscape

Research A publishes a general purpose app for Cytoscape, e.g., to perform a layout or cluster algorithm.

Research B installs the app and executes it in their local instance of Cytoscape.

Key Points:

  • Submitting a Cytoscape app is trivial.
  • B doesn't have to worry about anything other than having Cytoscape.

Script-apps for Cytoscape

Use case #1: Cloud-hosted notebooks

Research A publishes a Cytoscape-ready notebook online via a public URL, e.g., reproducible workflows.

Research B visits the site via their browser and executes it in coordination with their local instance of Cytoscape.

Key Points:

  • Publishing notebooks is becoming routine.
  • B doesn’t have to worry about R/Python versions or anything other than having a browser and Cytoscape.
